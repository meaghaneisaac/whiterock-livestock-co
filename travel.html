Travel

I love to travel. I have a large family (there's six of us), so before I was 14 we mainly camped in Ontario. When I was 14, the summer after grade 9, we took a road trip to stay in Prince Edward Island for a week. It was my first time leaving Ontario. From then on, I have contracted the travel bug. I love to go new places, especially historic places, and making new memories. I left the country for the first time in grade 10, and I have maps and globes everywhere at home ready to plan another trip. I am really excited to get my placement for my exchange in 3A, and I hope COVID-19 doesn't end up shutting it down. I'm super excited for the borders to open up so I can go on another trip!
